Coral

branding, design, front-end development, entrepreneurship, & teaching

My last three semesters of college, I co-founded and led Coral, a web design & development agency and learning group founded to teach students for students to design, build websites and software, and give them an opportunity work on team projects. We held weekly worknights and weekly more structured lessons. Students were encouraged to look for projects that interest them and lead them when capable, always with the help and support of the entire group.
